# Building Design Intelligence

AI agents don't have taste. They guess at colors, spacing, hierarchy. **Rafters** encodes a designer's judgment into queryable data so AI doesn't guess -- it reads decisions.

## The Three Layers

The architecture is built on three layers:

1. **What** (Components) -- `packages/ui` with JSDoc intelligence metadata
2. **Where** (Tokens) -- `packages/design-tokens` with dependency graph
3. **Why** (Decisions) -- Do/never patterns, cognitive load scores

<Card title="Key Insight">

The AI learns *what* to do, **because of how** it works, and ~~sometimes~~ **why** it matters.

- Every component has [cognitive load ratings](https://example.com/docs)
- Tokens form a `dependency graph` with 240+ relationships
- Decisions are encoded, not documented

> The decisions are already made by the designer. The AI just reads them.

</Card>
